About ISMMACS

The Indian Society for Mathematical Modeling and Computer Simulation (ISMMACS), established in 1993 with its headquarters at IIT Kanpur, has emerged as a pioneering institution committed to advancing the frontiers of mathematical modeling and computational science. Over the decades, ISMMACS has played a transformative role in promoting cutting-edge research and innovation across diverse domains of science, engineering, and technology. The society actively nurtures interdisciplinary collaboration, bridging theoretical advancements with real-world applications that address complex societal and industrial challenges. Through its flagship conferences, thematic workshops, and academic meets, ISMMACS offers an intellectually stimulating platform for scholars, scientists, and professionals to exchange ideas, foster partnerships, and drive scientific excellence. Moreover, the society takes pride in celebrating academic and professional achievements by conferring prestigious awards, medals, and fellowships, thereby encouraging a culture of recognition and excellence within the research community.

For more information, visit the official website: https://ismmacs.org.in

    About Gandhinagar & Tourism

    Gandhinagar, the capital city of Gujarat, is known for its greenery, wide roads, and planned layout. It is home to many academic institutions and government establishments, offering a calm and disciplined environment.

    Nearby attractions include:

    • Akshardham Temple – A majestic spiritual-cultural campus.
    • Indroda Nature Park – Known as India’s Jurassic Park.
    • Sabarmati Riverfront – A scenic place to relax and enjoy the beauty of nature in Ahmedabad.
    • Adalaj Stepwell – A historical marvel reflecting Indo-Islamic architecture.

    The city offers a perfect mix of modern facilities and cultural richness. Visitors can also explore the vibrant local cuisine, handicrafts, and festivals.
