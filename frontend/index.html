About ISMMACS

The Indian Society for Mathematical Modeling and Computer Simulation (ISMMACS), established in 1993 with its headquarters at IIT Kanpur, has emerged as a pioneering institution committed to advancing the frontiers of mathematical modeling and computational science.

ISMMACS promotes cutting-edge research across diverse domains of science, engineering, and technology. The society bridges theoretical advancements with real-world applications, hosts flagship conferences and workshops, and celebrates academic achievements through awards and fellowships.

For more information, visit the official website: https://ismmacs.org.in

    About Gandhinagar & Tourism

    Gandhinagar, the capital city of Gujarat, is known for its greenery, wide roads, and planned layout.
